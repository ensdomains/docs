This is a vote to elect three stewards for the Meta-Governance Working Group for the Term 7 (July 1, 2026 - June 30, 2027).

This election will be using ranked-choice voting, with Copeland (a very balanced algorithm for computing collective preferences) and Shielded Voting (your vote will be private during the voting window and revealed afterward).

To vote, rank each candidate in your order of preference.

At the conclusion of voting, the three highest-ranked candidates will be elected as stewards of the Meta-Governance Working Group for Term 7.

A list of candidates, with links to their nomination statement and Twitter profile, is included below. The list is presented in the order in which candidates submitted their nominations in the ENS DAO governance forum.
